Pizza On The Grill

Pizza on the grill is a fantastic way to make pizza at home. The heat of a hot grill is a perfect match for a professional pizza oven. Learn the tricks for making and grilling pizza from scratch with this recipe. Feel free to use your favorite toppings, but keep in mind not to overload the pizza.

Ingredients

  • oil
  • fresh basil
  • dry yeast
  • black olives
  • mozzarella
  • red peppers
  • sugar
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Make dough: Pour warm water into a large bowl; dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water.
  3. place dough in a well-oiled bowl and cover with damp cloth.
  4. preheat outdoor grill for high heat.
  5. place dough in hot grill. Put basil, tomato, olives and pepper on dough.
  6. Close the lid until the cheese melts. Remove from grill and set aside to cool for a few minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
